Adding Catogories

Catogories

You must have at least one Main Category to add sub categories and books under main one. Note - You can't remove Main Category once it's created.

In Sub Catogories, You can do anything you want. It's fully customizable.

Navigations

Tap on Main Categeroy & Sub Category cards to move around your library

Adding Books to your library

It's very Easy!

Just enter details to add prompt page and tap ADD to add book to your library. Please mind the ISBN number. It's very Important.

If you are borrowing book/s follow the same steps.

Borrowing & Lending Books

You can get them done by with few taps

In Borrowing, just follow same steps as adding books with additional details available after you select borrowing option (check previous slide image.)

On Lending tap the Lend button given on every book card and fill the details.

Favorites, Bookmarks & Readings

They all come with the handy Book Card

You can add a book to Favorites list, add a Bookmark to a book or even mark a book as a Reading Book. These all functions are on every book card you entered.
